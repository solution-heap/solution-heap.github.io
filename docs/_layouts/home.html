A bit about us

Exams are often more difficult than homework and it doesn't make things easier when you still struggle with a solution memo because you don't understand how some questions were solved.

Solutionheap allows the student to curate math questions from past exam papers so they can focus on the topics they most struggle with. Each question has a hint on how to solve it and every solution explains the steps in a simple and approachable way.
