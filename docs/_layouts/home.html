Our history

The idea of Solutionheap came about while helping my brother study for his grade 11 mathematics exams.

I saw that while there was good understanding with homework questions, it wasn't so when it came to exams.The questions from exams are often more complicated and are aren't extensively covered during normal classes, especially in public schools.
